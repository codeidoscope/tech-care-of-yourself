# tech-care-of-yourself
Tools and strategies for better mental health in tech

["Tech" Care of Yourself: strategies for better mental health in tech](https://8thlight.com/blog/marion-lv/2019/06/18/tech-care-of-yourself.html) The blog post that started it all
## Tools
- [Self Compassion](https://self-compassion.org/) - The website of Kristin Neff a leader in the field. She has excellent books and audiobooks to help you understand the principles of self-compassion and apply them to your life
- [Carol Dweck's Ted Talk](https://www.ted.com/talks/carol_dweck_the_power_of_believing_that_you_can_improve) - To learn and understand more about the fixed and growth mindsets.

## Strategies
- [Codelogs Template](https://github.com/codeidoscope/codelogs-template) - To start your own logs
- [Dev.To](https://dev.to/) A brilliant dev community to get started with blogging
- [Asking the right questions](https://jvns.ca/blog/good-questions/) - An excellent post by Julia Evans
